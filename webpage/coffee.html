BEST COFFEE

Choffy is cocoa beans (cacao) that have been roasted and ground in our unique way, then packaged so that you can brew it into a healthy, delicious brewed cacao drink! The heart of Choffy lies in the careful selection of premium cacao beans from our partner estates across the globe.


Hand Selected Beans

The heart of Choffy lies in the careful selection of premium cacao beans from our partner estates across the globe. Each bean is chosen with care, reflecting a commitment to quality, authenticity, and a personal touch. In a world of mass production, choose the unique charm of Choffy, curated just for you.


Ground and Packaged

As a vital moment of our Choffy-making process, we grind and package the Choffy to bring the pure bliss of brewed chocolate to you.
